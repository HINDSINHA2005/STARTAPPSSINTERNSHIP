ABES OneView

Academic Management Web Application

ABES OneView is a full-stack web application designed to centralize academic information for students and faculty. It allows students to view semester results, sessional and practical marks, assignment submissions, quiz scores, and approved achievements. Faculty members can upload marks, assignments, and quizzes, while admins manage users, subjects, and approvals. The system improves transparency, performance tracking, and academic record management.

eVoter

Online Voting System

eVoter is a secure web-based voting system designed to conduct digital elections efficiently. It allows registered users to cast votes online while ensuring vote authenticity and preventing duplicate voting. The admin panel manages candidates, voters, and election results. This project focuses on improving accessibility, reducing manual effort, and maintaining transparency in the voting process.
